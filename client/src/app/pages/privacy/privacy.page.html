Information We Collect

We collect account details (such as name and email), booking activity, and technical usage data required to deliver secure sign-in, reservations, and service improvements.

Purpose

How We Use Data

We use your data to process bookings, send confirmations, provide support, prevent fraud, and personalize your event discovery experience.

Control

Your Privacy Rights

You can request account data updates, ask for deletion where allowed, and opt out of non-essential communication at any time.

Data Retention and Security

  • We retain data only as long as needed for legal, operational, and support requirements.
  • Access to sensitive data is restricted and monitored.
  • Security controls are regularly reviewed to protect against unauthorized access.
